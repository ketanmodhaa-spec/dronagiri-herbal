We want you to be happy with your purchase. Because our products are herbal cosmetics applied to the body, hygiene and safety rules apply to returns. Please read this policy carefully.

## Return Window

You may request a return within **7 days** of delivery, subject to the conditions below.

## What Can Be Returned

✓ **Eligible for return:**
- Products that arrive **damaged, defective, or leaking**
- **Wrong product** delivered (different from what you ordered)
- Products that are **unopened, unused, and in original sealed packaging**

✗ **NOT eligible for return (for hygiene and safety reasons):**
- Products that have been **opened or used**
- Products without original packaging or seal
- Products returned after the 7-day window
- Products damaged due to misuse or improper storage by the customer

This hygiene exclusion is standard for cosmetic and personal-care products and protects all our customers.

## How to Request a Return

1. Contact us within 7 days of delivery at store@dronagiriherbal.in or WhatsApp +91 94290 29840.
2. Provide your order number and a photo of the product (especially if damaged or wrong).
3. We will review and respond within 2 business days.
4. If approved, we will guide you on returning the product.

## Return Shipping Costs

- If the return is due to **our error** (damaged, defective, or wrong product) — **we bear the return shipping cost.**
- If the return is for **any other reason** — the customer bears the return shipping cost.

## Refunds

- Once we receive and inspect the returned product, we will process your refund within 5–7 business days.
- Refunds are issued to your **original payment method** for online payments.
- For COD orders, refunds are issued via bank transfer or UPI.
- Refund timelines after processing depend on your bank (typically 5–7 working days).

## Cancellations

- You may cancel your order **before it is dispatched** at no charge.
- To cancel, contact us as soon as possible at +91 94290 29840.
- Once dispatched, an order cannot be cancelled but may be eligible for return under this policy.
- For prepaid orders cancelled before dispatch, a full refund is issued.

## Damaged or Wrong Products

If your order arrives damaged or incorrect:

- Take a photo before opening fully if damage is visible.
- Contact us within 48 hours of delivery.
- We will arrange a replacement or full refund at no cost to you.
