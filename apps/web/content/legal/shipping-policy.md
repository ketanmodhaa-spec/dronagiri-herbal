## Where We Ship

We currently ship across India.

## Order Processing Time

- Orders are processed and dispatched within **2–4 business days** of confirmation.
- Orders are not dispatched or delivered on Sundays or public holidays.
- During festivals or high-demand periods, processing may take slightly longer — we will keep you informed.

## Delivery Time

- Estimated delivery is **5–10 business days** from dispatch, depending on your location.
- Metro cities typically receive orders faster than remote areas.
- Delivery times are estimates, not guarantees, and may be affected by courier delays, weather, or circumstances beyond our control.

## Shipping Charges

- **Free shipping** on orders above ₹499.
- Orders below this value attract a flat shipping charge of ₹49, shown at checkout before payment.

## Cash on Delivery (COD)

- COD is available across India.
- There is no extra charge for COD orders.
- Please keep the exact amount ready at the time of delivery.

## Order Tracking

- Once dispatched, you will receive a tracking number via WhatsApp/SMS/email.
- You can track your order anytime using the tracking link we send, or on our Website — no login required.

## Failed Delivery

- If delivery fails because no one is available or the address is incorrect, our courier will attempt redelivery.
- After repeated failed attempts, the order may be returned to us. We will contact you to arrange redelivery (charges may apply) or a refund.

## Shipping Partner

We ship via leading national couriers — including Delhivery, India Post and Shiprocket partners — selected based on your delivery PIN code.
