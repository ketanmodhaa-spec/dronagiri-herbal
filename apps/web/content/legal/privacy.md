Dronagiri Herbal ("we", "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This policy explains what we collect, why, and your rights — in line with the Information Technology Act, 2000 and the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDP Act). [CONFIRM DPDP compliance with lawyer]

## Information We Collect

**You give us directly:**
- Name, phone number, email address (email is optional)
- Delivery address
- Order history

**Collected automatically:**
- Device and browser information
- Pages visited and actions taken on the Website (analytics)
- A session identifier stored in a cookie

**We do NOT collect or store:**
- Your full payment card details — these are handled directly by our payment partner Razorpay, who is PCI-DSS compliant. We never see or store your card number.

## How We Use Your Information

- To process and deliver your orders
- To send you order updates (confirmation, shipping, delivery) via WhatsApp, SMS, or email
- To provide customer support
- To improve our products and Website
- To send occasional offers and product updates — **only if you have given consent**, and you can opt out anytime
- To comply with legal and tax obligations

## Sharing Your Information

We share your information only with:

- **Delivery partners** — to ship your order (name, address, phone)
- **Razorpay** — to process payments
- **WhatsApp/SMS/email providers** — to send you order updates
- **Government authorities** — only where legally required

We do **not** sell your personal data to anyone.

## Data Security

- Phone numbers used for analytics are stored in hashed (anonymised) form.
- Passwords, if you set one, are stored using strong one-way encryption (bcrypt).
- All data is transmitted over secure HTTPS connections.
- Access to personal data is restricted to authorised personnel only.

## Cookies

We use a small number of cookies:

- A session cookie to keep your cart and login working
- Analytics cookies (Google Analytics) to understand how the Website is used

You can disable cookies in your browser, though some features may not work.

## Your Rights

Under the DPDP Act, you have the right to:

- Access the personal data we hold about you
- Request correction of inaccurate data
- Request deletion of your data
- Withdraw consent for marketing communications

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at store@dronagiriherbal.in.
