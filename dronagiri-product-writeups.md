> ## ⚠️ COMPLIANCE NOTE — read before publishing
>
> These are **cosmetics**, not medicines. Indian law (Drugs & Cosmetics
> Act) prohibits medicinal/therapeutic claims on cosmetic products.
>
> **SAFE language (used throughout):**
> - "helps reduce..." / "helps maintain..."
> - "traditionally used for..."
> - "leaves hair feeling..."
> - "nourishes / conditions / cleanses"
>
> **BANNED language (never use):**
> - "cures" / "treats" / "heals" / "medicine" / "therapy"
> - "stops hair fall" → say "helps reduce hair fall"
> - "removes dandruff" → say "helps control dandruff"
> - any claim to treat a disease or medical condition
>
> Every [SARITA: ...] tag = a fact only she can confirm.
> Every claim must survive the lawyer review.

**Ingredients (EN):** [SARITA confirms the real blend]

2. Mix with curd, buttermilk, or plain water into a smooth paste.
